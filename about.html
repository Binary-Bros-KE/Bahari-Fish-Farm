About Us

Vision, Mission, & Core Values

Vision

To be a leader in sustainable aquaculture in Kenya, providing high-quality fish and chicken products while promoting environmental stewardship.

Mission

At Bahari Fish Farm, we believe that seafood should be both sustainable and accessible. Our mission is to deliver exceptional seafood while promoting environmentally friendly practices. We are committed to educating our customers about the importance of sustainable fishing and the benefits of choosing responsibly sourced products.

Quality

Dedication to producing high-quality fish products that meet the highest standards of freshness and safety.

Health & Nutrition

Promoting the health benefits of fish consumption and providing nutritious, sustainable options for consumers.

Integrity

Upholding transparency and honesty in all operations, from sourcing to customer interactions.

Responsibility

Acknowledging our role in the food supply chain and striving to contribute positively to food security and local economies.

Customer Focus

Prioritizing customer satisfaction through exceptional service, quality products, and open communication.

About Bahari Fish: Who We are

Founded in 2014, Bahari Fish Farm has grown from a small local business into a trusted name in the seafood industry. Our journey began with a simple passion for providing the freshest, highest-quality fish to our community.

Over the years, we’ve built strong relationships with local fishermen and sustainable fisheries, ensuring that our products are not only delicious but also responsibly sourced.
